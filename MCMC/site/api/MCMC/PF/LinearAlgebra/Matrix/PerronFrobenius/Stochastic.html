    Perron-Frobenius Theorem for Column-Stochastic Matrices.

    An irreducible, non-negative matrix with column sums equal to 1 (i.e., column-stochastic) has a Perron root of 1, and there exists a unique (up to scaling) strictly positive eigenvector v for this eigenvalue. This stationary vector is unique when constrained to the standard simplex.

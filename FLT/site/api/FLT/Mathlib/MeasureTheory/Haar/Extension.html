    Haar measures on group extensions #

    In this file, if 1 ‚Üí A ‚Üí B ‚Üí C ‚Üí 1 is a short exact sequence of topological groups, we construct a Haar measure on B from Haar measures on A and B.

    Main definitions #

    • TopologicalGroup.IsSES œÜ œà: A predicate stating that œÜ is a closed embedding, œà is an open quotient map, and œÜ.range = œà.ker.
    • TopologicalGroup.IsSES.inducedMeasure: The Haar measure on B induced by Haar measures on A and C.

    Main results #

    • TopologicalGroup.IsSES.isHaarMeasure_inducedMeasure: inducedMeasure is a Haar measure.
    • TopologicalGroup.IsSES.not_injOn_of_inducedMeasure_gt: A sufficiently large open subset of B cannot be a fundamental domain.

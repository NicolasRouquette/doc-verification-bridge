    Perfect Secrecy: Internal proofs #

    Auxiliary lemmas for perfect secrecy:

    • Equivalence of the conditional-probability and independence formulations
    • Both directions of the ciphertext indistinguishability characterization ([KatzLindell2020], Lemma 2.5)
    • Shannon's key-space bound ([KatzLindell2020], Theorem 2.12)

    Perfect secrecy is equivalent to message-ciphertext independence. The two formulations are related by multiplying/dividing by marginal(c).

    The Global Langlands Conjectures for GL(n) over the rationals. #

    First sub-goal: definition of an automorphic form. #

    I've made the design decision of working with the functor Matrix.GeneralLinearGroup (Fin n) as our implementation of the GL_n functor. There's notation GL (Fin n) for this.

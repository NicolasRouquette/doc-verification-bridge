    Herbrand quotient of short exact sequences of representations #

    This file shows that the Herbrand quotient is multiplicative, in the sense that if 0 ⟶ A ⟶ B ⟶ C ⟶ 0 is a short exact sequence of representations, then the Herbrand quotient of B is the product of the Herbrand quotients of A and C.

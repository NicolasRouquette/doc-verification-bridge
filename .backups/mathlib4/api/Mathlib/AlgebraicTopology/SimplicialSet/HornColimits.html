    Horns as colimits #

    In this file, we express horns as colimits:

    • horns in Œî[2] are pushouts of two copies of Œî[1];
    • horns in Œî[n] are multicoequalizers of copies of the standard simplex of dimension n-1 (a dedicated API is provided for inner horns in Œî[3]).

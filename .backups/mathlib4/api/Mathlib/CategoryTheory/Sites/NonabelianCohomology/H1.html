    The cohomology of a sheaf of groups in degree 1

    In this file, we shall define the cohomology in degree 1 of a sheaf of groups (TODO).

    Currently, given a presheaf of groups G : C·µí·µñ ‚•§ GrpCat and a family of objects U : I ‚Üí C, we define 1-cochains/1-cocycles/H^1 with values in G over U. (This definition neither requires the assumption that G is a sheaf, nor that U covers the terminal object.)

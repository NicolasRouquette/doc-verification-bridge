    Exponentiable morphisms #

    We define an exponentiable morphism f : I ‚ü∂ J to be a morphism with a functorial choice of pullbacks, given by ChosenPullbacksAlong f, together with a right adjoint to the pullback functor ChosenPullbacksAlong.pulback f : Over J ‚•§ Over I. We call this right adjoint the pushforward functor along f.

    Main results #

    • The identity morphisms are exponentiable.
    • The composition of exponentiable morphisms is exponentiable.

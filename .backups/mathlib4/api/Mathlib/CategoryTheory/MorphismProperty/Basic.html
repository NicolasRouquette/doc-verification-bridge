    Properties of morphisms #

    We provide the basic framework for talking about properties of morphisms. The following meta-property is defined

    • RespectsLeft P Q: P respects the property Q on the left if P f ‚Üí P (i ‚â´ f) where i satisfies Q.
    • RespectsRight P Q: P respects the property Q on the right if P f ‚Üí P (f ‚â´ i) where i satisfies Q.
    • Respects: P respects Q if P respects Q both on the left and on the right.

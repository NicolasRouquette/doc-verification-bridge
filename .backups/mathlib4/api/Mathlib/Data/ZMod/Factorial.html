    Facts about factorials in ZMod #

    We collect facts about factorials in context of modular arithmetic.

    Main statements #

    • ZMod.cast_descFactorial: For natural numbers n and p, where n is less than or equal to p the descending factorial of (p - 1) taken n times modulo p equals (-1) ^ n * n!.

    The complex numbers #

    The complex numbers are modelled as ‚Ñù^2 in the obvious way and it is shown that they form a field of characteristic zero. For the result that the complex numbers are algebraically closed, see Complex.isAlgClosed in Mathlib.Analysis.Complex.Polynomial.Basic.

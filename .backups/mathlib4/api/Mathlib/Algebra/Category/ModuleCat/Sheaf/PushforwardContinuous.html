    Pushforward of sheaves of modules #

    Assume that categories C and D are equipped with Grothendieck topologies, and that F : C ‚•§ D is a continuous functor. Then, if œÜ : S ‚ü∂ (F.sheafPushforwardContinuous RingCat.{u} J K).obj R is a morphism of sheaves of rings, we construct the pushforward functor pushforward œÜ : SheafOfModules.{v} R ‚•§ SheafOfModules.{v} S, and we show that they interact with the composition of morphisms similarly as pseudofunctors.

    The forgetful functor from (commutative) (additive) groups preserves filtered colimits. #

    Forgetful functors from algebraic categories usually don't preserve colimits. However, they tend to preserve filtered colimits.

    In this file, we start with a small filtered category J and a functor F : J ‚•§ GrpCat. We show that the colimit of F ‚ãô forget‚ÇÇ GrpCat MonCat (in MonCat) carries the structure of a group, thereby showing that the forgetful functor forget‚ÇÇ GrpCat MonCat preserves filtered colimits. In particular, this implies that forget GrpCat preserves filtered colimits. Similarly for AddGrpCat, CommGrpCat and AddCommGrpCat.

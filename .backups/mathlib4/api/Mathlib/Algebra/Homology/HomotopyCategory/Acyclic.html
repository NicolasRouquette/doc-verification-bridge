    The triangulated subcategory of acyclic complex in the homotopy category #

    In this file, we define the triangulated subcategory HomotopyCategory.subcategoryAcyclic C of the homotopy category of cochain complexes in an abelian category C. In the lemma HomotopyCategory.quasiIso_eq_subcategoryAcyclic_W we obtain that the class of quasiisomorphisms HomotopyCategory.quasiIso C (ComplexShape.up ‚Ñ§) consists of morphisms whose cone belongs to the triangulated subcategory HomotopyCategory.subcategoryAcyclic C of acyclic complexes.

    Basic results on subgroups #

    We prove basic results on the definitions of subgroups. The bundled subgroups use bundled monoid homomorphisms.

    Special thanks goes to Amelia Livingston and Yury Kudryashov for their help and inspiration.

    Main definitions #

    Notation used here:

    • G N are Groups

    • A is an AddGroup

    • H K are Subgroups of G or AddSubgroups of A

    • x is an element of type G or type A

    • f g : N ‚Üí* G are group homomorphisms

    • s k are sets of elements of type G

    Definitions in the file:

    • Subgroup.prod H K : the product of subgroups H, K of groups G, N respectively, H √ó K is a subgroup of G √ó N

    Implementation notes #

    Subgroup inclusion is denoted ‚â§ rather than ‚äÜ, although ‚àà is defined as membership of a subgroup's underlying set.

    Functors #

    This module provides additional lemmas, definitions, and instances for Functors.

    Main definitions #

    • Functor.Const Œ± is the functor that sends all types to Œ±.
    • Functor.AddConst Œ± is Functor.Const Œ± but for when Œ± has an additive structure.
    • Functor.Comp F G for functors F and G is the functor composition of F and G.
    • Liftp and Liftr respectively lift predicates and relations on a type Œ± to F Œ±. Terms of F Œ± are considered to, in some sense, contain values of type Œ±.

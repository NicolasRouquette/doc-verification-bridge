    Bases of dual vector spaces #

    The dual space of an $R$-module $M$ is the $R$-module of $R$-linear maps $M \to R$. This file concerns bases on dual vector spaces.

    Main definitions #

    • Bases:
      • Basis.toDual produces the map M ‚Üí‚Çó[R] Dual R M associated to a basis for an R-module M.
      • Basis.toDualEquiv is the equivalence M ‚âÉ‚Çó[R] Dual R M associated to a finite basis.
      • Basis.dualBasis is a basis for Dual R M given a finite basis for M.
      • Module.DualBases e Œµ is the proposition that the families e of vectors and Œµ of dual vectors have the characteristic properties of a basis and a dual.

    Main results #

    • Bases:
      • Module.DualBases.basis and Module.DualBases.coe_basis: if e and Œµ form a dual pair, then e is a basis.
      • Module.DualBases.coe_dualBasis: if e and Œµ form a dual pair, then Œµ is a basis.

    Pointwise convergence of indicator functions #

    In this file, we prove the equivalence of three different ways to phrase that the indicator functions of sets converge pointwise.

    Main results #

    For A a set, (As·µ¢) an indexed collection of sets, under mild conditions, the following are equivalent:

    (a) the indicator functions of As·µ¢ tend to the indicator function of A pointwise;

    (b) for every x, we eventually have that x ‚àà As·µ¢ holds iff x ‚àà A holds;

    (c) Tendsto As _ <| Filter.pi (pure <| ¬∑ ‚àà A).

    The results stating these in the case when the indicators take values in a Fr√©chet space are:

    • tendsto_indicator_const_iff_forall_eventually is the equivalence (a) ‚Üî (b);
    • tendsto_indicator_const_iff_tendsto_pi_pure is the equivalence (a) ‚Üî (c).

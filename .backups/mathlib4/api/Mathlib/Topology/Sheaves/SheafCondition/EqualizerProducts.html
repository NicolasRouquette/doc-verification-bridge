    The sheaf condition in terms of an equalizer of products #

    Here we set up the machinery for the "usual" definition of the sheaf condition, e.g. as in https://stacks.math.columbia.edu/tag/0072 in terms of an equalizer diagram where the two objects are ‚àè·∂ú F.obj (U i) and ‚àè·∂ú F.obj (U i) ‚äì (U j).

    We show that this sheaf condition is equivalent to the "pairwise intersections" sheaf condition when the presheaf is valued in a category with products, and thereby equivalent to the default sheaf condition.

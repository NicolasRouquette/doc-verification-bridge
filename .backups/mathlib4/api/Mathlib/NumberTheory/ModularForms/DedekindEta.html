    Dedekind eta function #

    Main definitions #

    • We define the Dedekind eta function as the infinite product Œ∑(z) = q ^ 1/24 * ‚àè' (1 - q ^ (n + 1)) where q = e ^ (2œÄiz) and z is in the upper half-plane. The product is taken over all non-negative integers n. We then show it is non-vanishing and differentiable on the upper half-plane. Lastly, we compute its logarithmic derivative and show that it is a multiple of the Eisenstein series E2.

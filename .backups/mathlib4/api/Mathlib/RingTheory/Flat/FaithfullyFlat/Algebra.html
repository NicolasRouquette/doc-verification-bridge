    Properties of faithfully flat algebras #

    An A-algebra B is faithfully flat if B is faithfully flat as an A-module. In this file we give equivalent characterizations of faithful flatness in the algebra case.

    Main results #

    Let B be a faithfully flat A-algebra:

    • Ideal.comap_map_eq_self_of_faithfullyFlat: the contraction of the extension of any ideal of A to B is the ideal itself.
    • Module.FaithfullyFlat.tensorProduct_mk_injective: The natural map M ‚Üí‚Çó[A] B ‚äó[A] M is injective for any A-module M.
    • PrimeSpectrum.comap_surjective_of_faithfullyFlat: The map on prime spectra induced by a faithfully flat ring map is surjective. See also Ideal.exists_isPrime_liesOver_of_faithfullyFlat for a version stated in terms of Ideal.LiesOver.

    Conversely, let B be a flat A-algebra:

    • Module.FaithfullyFlat.of_comap_surjective: B is faithfully flat over A, if the induced map on prime spectra is surjective.
    • Module.FaithfullyFlat.of_flat_of_isLocalHom: flat + local implies faithfully flat

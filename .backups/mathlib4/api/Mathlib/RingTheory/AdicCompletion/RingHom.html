    Lift of ring homomorphisms to adic completions #

    Let R, S be rings, I be an ideal of S. In this file we prove that a compatible family of ring homomorphisms from a ring R to S ‚ß∏ I ^ n can be lifted to a ring homomorphism R ‚Üí+* AdicCompletion I S. If S is I-adically complete, then this compatible family of ring homomorphisms can be lifted to a ring homomorphism R ‚Üí+* S.

    Main definitions #

    • IsAdicComplete.liftRingHom: if R is I-adically complete, then a compatible family of ring maps S ‚Üí+* R ‚ß∏ I ^ n can be lifted to a unique ring map S ‚Üí+* R. Together with mk_liftRingHom_apply and eq_liftRingHom, it gives the universal property of R being I-adically complete.

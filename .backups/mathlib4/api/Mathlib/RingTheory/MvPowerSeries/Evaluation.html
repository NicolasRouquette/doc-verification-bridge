    Evaluation of multivariate power series #

    Let œÉ, R, S be types, with CommRing R, CommRing S. One assumes that IsTopologicalRing R and IsUniformAddGroup R, and that S is a complete and separated topological R-algebra, with IsLinearTopology S S, which means there is a basis of neighborhoods of 0 consisting of ideals.

    Given œÜ : R ‚Üí+* S, a : œÉ ‚Üí S, and f : MvPowerSeries œÉ R, MvPowerSeries.eval‚ÇÇ f œÜ a is the evaluation of the multivariate power series f at a. It f is (the coercion of) a polynomial, it coincides with the evaluation of that polynomial. Otherwise, it is defined by density from polynomials; its values are irrelevant unless œÜ is continuous and a satisfies two conditions bundled in MvPowerSeries.HasEval a :

    • for all s : œÉ, a s is topologically nilpotent, meaning that (a s) ^ n tends to 0 when n tends to infinity
    • when a s tends to zero for the filter of cofinite subsets of œÉ.

    Under Continuous œÜ and HasEval a, the following lemmas furnish the properties of evaluation:

    • MvPowerSeries.eval‚ÇÇHom: the evaluation of multivariate power series, as a ring morphism,
    • MvPowerSeries.aeval: the evaluation map as an algebra morphism
    • MvPowerSeries.uniformContinuous_eval‚ÇÇ: uniform continuity of the evaluation
    • MvPowerSeries.continuous_eval‚ÇÇ: continuity of the evaluation
    • MvPowerSeries.eval‚ÇÇ_eq_tsum: the evaluation is given by the sum of its monomials, evaluated.

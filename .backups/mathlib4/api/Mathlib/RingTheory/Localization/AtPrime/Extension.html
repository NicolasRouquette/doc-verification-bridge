    Primes in an extension of localization at prime #

    Let R ‚äÜ S be an extension of Dedekind domains and p be a prime ideal of R. Let R‚Çö be the localization of R at the complement of p and S‚Çö the localization of S at the (image) of the complement of p.

    In this file, we study the relation between the (nonzero) prime ideals of S‚Çö and the prime ideals of S above p. In particular, we prove that (under suitable conditions) they are in bijection and that the residual degree and ramification index are preserved by this bijection.

    Main definitions and results #

    • IsLocalization.AtPrime.mem_primesOver_of_isPrime: The nonzero prime ideals of S‚Çö are primes over the maximal ideal of R‚Çö.

    • IsLocalization.AtPrime.equivQuotientMapOfIsMaximal: S ‚ß∏ P ‚âÉ+* S‚Çö ‚ß∏ P¬∑S‚Çö where P is a maximal ideal of S above p.

    • IsDedekindDomain.primesOverEquivPrimesOver: the bijection between the primes over p in S and the primes over the maximal ideal of R‚Çö in S‚Çö.

    • IsDedekindDomain.primesOverEquivPrimesOver_inertiagDeg_eq: the bijection primesOverEquivPrimesOver preserves the inertia degree.

    • IsDedekindDomain.primesOverEquivPrimesOver_ramificationIdx_eq: the bijection primesOverEquivPrimesOver preserves the ramification index.

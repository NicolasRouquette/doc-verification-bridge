    Akra-Bazzi theorem: the sum transform #

    We develop further preliminaries required for the theorem, up to the sum transform.

    Main definitions and results #

    • AkraBazziRecurrence.p: the unique Akra‚ÄìBazzi exponent characterized by $\sum_i a_i\,(b_i)^p = 1$ and supporting analytical lemmas such as continuity and injectivity of the defining sum.

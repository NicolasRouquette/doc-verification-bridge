    Duality for finite abelian groups #

    Let G be a finite abelian group.

    For M a commutative monoid that has enough nth roots of unity, where n is the exponent of G, the main results in this file are:

    • CommGroup.exists_apply_ne_one_of_hasEnoughRootsOfUnity: Homomorphisms G ‚Üí* MÀ£ separate elements of G.

    • CommGroup.monoidHomMonoidHomEquiv: G is isomorphic to its double dual (G ‚Üí* MÀ£) ‚Üí* MÀ£.

    The arctan function. #

    Inequalities, identities and Real.tan as an OpenPartialHomeomorph between (-(Ï€ / 2), Ï€ / 2) and the whole line.

    The result of arctan x + arctan y is given by arctan_add, arctan_add_eq_add_pi or arctan_add_eq_sub_pi depending on whether x * y < 1 and 0 < x. As an application of arctan_add we give four Machin-like formulas (linear combinations of arctangents equal to Ï€ / 4 = arctan 1), including John Machin's original one at four_mul_arctan_inv_5_sub_arctan_inv_239.

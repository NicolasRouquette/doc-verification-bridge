    The Proper Lorentz Group #

    The proper Lorentz group is the subgroup of the Lorentz group with determinant 1.

    We define the give a series of lemmas related to the determinant of the Lorentz group.

    (↑Λ).det = 1 ∨ (↑Λ).det = -1

    Negation and addition formulae for nonsingular points in affine coordinates #

    Let W be a Weierstrass curve over a field F with coefficients a·µ¢. The nonsingular affine points on W can be given negation and addition operations defined by a secant-and-tangent process.

    • Given a nonsingular affine point P, its negation -P is defined to be the unique third nonsingular point of intersection between W and the vertical line through P. Explicitly, if P is (x, y), then -P is (x, -y - a‚ÇÅx - a‚ÇÉ).

    • Given two nonsingular affine points P and Q, their addition P + Q is defined to be the negation of the unique third nonsingular point of intersection between W and the line L through P and Q. Explicitly, let P be (x‚ÇÅ, y‚ÇÅ) and let Q be (x‚ÇÇ, y‚ÇÇ).

      • If x‚ÇÅ = x‚ÇÇ and y‚ÇÅ = -y‚ÇÇ - a‚ÇÅx‚ÇÇ - a‚ÇÉ, then L is vertical.
      • If x‚ÇÅ = x‚ÇÇ and y‚ÇÅ ‚â† -y‚ÇÇ - a‚ÇÅx‚ÇÇ - a‚ÇÉ, then L is the tangent of W at P = Q, and has slope ‚Ñì := (3x‚ÇÅ¬≤ + 2a‚ÇÇx‚ÇÅ + a‚ÇÑ - a‚ÇÅy‚ÇÅ) / (2y‚ÇÅ + a‚ÇÅx‚ÇÅ + a‚ÇÉ).
      • Otherwise x‚ÇÅ ‚â† x‚ÇÇ, then L is the secant of W through P and Q, and has slope ‚Ñì := (y‚ÇÅ - y‚ÇÇ) / (x‚ÇÅ - x‚ÇÇ).

      In the last two cases, the X-coordinate of P + Q is then the unique third solution of the equation obtained by substituting the line Y = ‚Ñì(X - x‚ÇÅ) + y‚ÇÅ into the Weierstrass equation, and can be written down explicitly as x := ‚Ñì¬≤ + a‚ÇÅ‚Ñì - a‚ÇÇ - x‚ÇÅ - x‚ÇÇ by inspecting the coefficients of X¬≤. The Y-coordinate of P + Q, after applying the final negation that maps Y to -Y - a‚ÇÅX - a‚ÇÉ, is precisely y := -(‚Ñì(x - x‚ÇÅ) + y‚ÇÅ) - a‚ÇÅx - a‚ÇÉ.

    This file defines polynomials associated to negation and addition of nonsingular affine points, including slopes of non-vertical lines. The actual group law on nonsingular points in affine coordinates will be defined in Mathlib/AlgebraicGeometry/EllipticCurve/Affine/Point.lean.

    Main definitions #

    • WeierstrassCurve.Affine.negY: the Y-coordinate of -P.
    • WeierstrassCurve.Affine.addX: the X-coordinate of P + Q.
    • WeierstrassCurve.Affine.negAddY: the Y-coordinate of -(P + Q).
    • WeierstrassCurve.Affine.addY: the Y-coordinate of P + Q.

    Main statements #

    • WeierstrassCurve.Affine.equation_neg: negation preserves the Weierstrass equation.
    • WeierstrassCurve.Affine.nonsingular_neg: negation preserves the nonsingular condition.
    • WeierstrassCurve.Affine.equation_add: addition preserves the Weierstrass equation.
    • WeierstrassCurve.Affine.nonsingular_add: addition preserves the nonsingular condition.

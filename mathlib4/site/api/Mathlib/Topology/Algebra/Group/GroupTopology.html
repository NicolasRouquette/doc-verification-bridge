    Lattice of group topologies #

    We define a type class GroupTopology Œ± which endows a group Œ± with a topology such that all group operations are continuous.

    Group topologies on a fixed group Œ± are ordered, by reverse inclusion. They form a complete lattice, with ‚ä• the discrete topology and ‚ä§ the indiscrete topology.

    Any function f : Œ± ‚Üí Œ≤ induces coinduced f : TopologicalSpace Œ± ‚Üí GroupTopology Œ≤.

    The additive version AddGroupTopology Œ± and corresponding results are provided as well.

        Group topologies on Œ≥ form a complete lattice, with ‚ä• the discrete topology and ‚ä§ the indiscrete topology.

        The supremum of two group topologies s and t is the infimum of the family of all group topologies contained in the intersection of s and t.

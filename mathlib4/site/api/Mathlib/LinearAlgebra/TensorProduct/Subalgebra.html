    Some results on tensor product of subalgebras #

    Linear maps induced by multiplication for subalgebras #

    Let R be a commutative ring, S be a commutative R-algebra. Let A and B be R-subalgebras in S (Subalgebra R S). We define some linear maps induced by the multiplication in S, which are mainly used in the definition of linearly disjointness.

    • Subalgebra.mulMap: the natural R-algebra homomorphism A ‚äó[R] B ‚Üí‚Çê[R] S induced by the multiplication in S, whose image is A ‚äî B (Subalgebra.mulMap_range).

    • Subalgebra.mulMap': the natural R-algebra homomorphism A ‚äó[R] B ‚Üí‚Çó[R] A ‚äî B induced by multiplication in S, which is surjective (Subalgebra.mulMap'_surjective).

    • Subalgebra.lTensorBot, Subalgebra.rTensorBot: the natural isomorphism of R-algebras between i(R) ‚äó[R] A and A, resp. A ‚äó[R] i(R) and A, induced by multiplication in S, here i : R ‚Üí S is the structure map. They generalize Algebra.TensorProduct.lid and Algebra.TensorProduct.rid, as i(R) is not necessarily isomorphic to R.

      They are Subalgebra versions of Submodule.lTensorOne and Submodule.rTensorOne.

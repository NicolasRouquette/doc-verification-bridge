    Quotients by submodules #

    • If p is a submodule of M, M ‚ß∏ p is the quotient of M with respect to p: that is, elements of M are identified if their difference is in p. This is itself a module.

    Main definitions #

    • Submodule.Quotient.restrictScalarsEquiv: The quotient of P as an S-submodule is the same as the quotient of P as an R-submodule,
    • Submodule.liftQ: lift a map M ‚Üí M‚ÇÇ to a map M ‚ß∏ p ‚Üí M‚ÇÇ if the kernel is contained in p
    • Submodule.mapQ: lift a map M ‚Üí M‚ÇÇ to a map M ‚ß∏ p ‚Üí M‚ÇÇ ‚ß∏ q if the image of p is contained in q

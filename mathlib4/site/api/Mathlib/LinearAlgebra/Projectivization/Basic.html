    Projective Spaces #

    This file contains the definition of the projectivization of a vector space over a field, as well as the bijection between said projectivization and the collection of all one dimensional subspaces of the vector space.

    Notation #

    ‚Ñô K V is localized notation for Projectivization K V, the projectivization of a K-vector space V.

    Constructing terms of ‚Ñô K V. #

    We have three ways to construct terms of ‚Ñô K V:

    • Projectivization.mk K v hv where v : V and hv : v ‚â† 0.
    • Projectivization.mk' K v where v : { w : V // w ‚â† 0 }.
    • Projectivization.mk'' H h where H : Submodule K V and h : finrank H = 1.

    Other definitions #

    • For v : ‚Ñô K V, v.submodule gives the corresponding submodule of V.
    • Projectivization.equivSubmodule is the equivalence between ‚Ñô K V and { H : Submodule K V // finrank H = 1 }.
    • For v : ‚Ñô K V, v.rep : V is a representative of v.

    Sums of squares #

    We introduce a predicate for sums of squares in a ring.

    Main declarations #

    • IsSumSq : R ‚Üí Prop: for a type R with addition, multiplication and a zero, an inductive predicate defining the property of being a sum of squares in R. 0 : R is a sum of squares and if S is a sum of squares, then, for all a : R, a * a + s is a sum of squares.
    • AddMonoid.sumSq R and Subsemiring.sumSq R: respectively the submonoid or subsemiring of sums of squares in an additive monoid or semiring R with multiplication.

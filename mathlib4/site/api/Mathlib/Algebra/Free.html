    Main definitions #

    • FreeMagma Œ±: free magma (structure with binary operation without any axioms) over alphabet Œ±, defined inductively, with traversable instance and decidable equality.
    • MagmaAssocQuotient Œ±: quotient of a magma Œ± by the associativity equivalence relation.
    • FreeSemigroup Œ±: free semigroup over alphabet Œ±, defined as a structure with two fields head : Œ± and tail : List Œ± (i.e. nonempty lists), with traversable instance and decidable equality.
    • FreeMagmaAssocQuotientEquiv Œ±: isomorphism between MagmaAssocQuotient (FreeMagma Œ±) and FreeSemigroup Œ±.
    • FreeMagma.lift: the universal property of the free magma, expressing its adjointness.

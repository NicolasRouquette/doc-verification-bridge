    The cochain complex of homomorphisms between cochain complexes

    If F and G are cochain complexes (indexed by ‚Ñ§) in a preadditive category, there is a cochain complex of abelian groups whose 0-cocycles identify to morphisms F ‚ü∂ G. Informally, in degree n, this complex shall consist of cochains of degree n from F to G, i.e. arbitrary families for morphisms F.X p ‚ü∂ G.X (p + n). This complex shall be denoted HomComplex F G.

    In order to avoid type-theoretic issues, a cochain of degree n : ‚Ñ§ (i.e. a term of type of Cochain F G n) shall be defined here as the data of a morphism F.X p ‚ü∂ G.X q for all triplets ‚ü®p, q, hpq‚ü© where p and q are integers and hpq : p + n = q. If Œ± : Cochain F G n, we shall define Œ±.v p q hpq : F.X p ‚ü∂ G.X q.

    We follow the signs conventions appearing in the introduction of [Brian Conrad's book Grothendieck duality and base change][conrad2000].

    Consequences of the homology sequence #

    Given a morphism œÜ : S‚ÇÅ ‚ü∂ S‚ÇÇ between two short exact sequences of homological complexes in an abelian category, we show the naturality of the homology sequence of S‚ÇÅ and S‚ÇÇ with respect to œÜ (see HomologicalComplex.HomologySequence.Œ¥_naturality).

    • eq_quo_mul_prod_pow_add_sum_rem_mul_prod_pow: Partial fraction decomposition for polynomials over a commutative ring R, the denomiator is a product of powers of monic pairwise coprime polynomials. The denominators are multiplied out on both sides and formally cancelled.
    • eq_quo_mul_prod_add_sum_rem_mul_prod: Partial fraction decomposition for polynomials over a commutative ring R, the denomiator is a product of monic pairwise coprime polynomials. The denominators are multiplied out on both sides and formally cancelled.
    • div_prod_eq_quo_add_sum_rem_div: Partial fraction decomposition for polynomials over an integral domain R, the denominator is a product of monic pairwise coprime polynomials. Division is done in a field K containing R[X].

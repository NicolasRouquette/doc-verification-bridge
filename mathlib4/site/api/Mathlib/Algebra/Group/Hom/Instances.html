    Instances on spaces of monoid and group morphisms #

    We endow the space of monoid morphisms M ‚Üí* N with a CommMonoid structure when the target is commutative, through pointwise multiplication, and with a CommGroup structure when the target is a commutative group. We also prove the same instances for additive situations.

    Since these structures permit morphisms of morphisms, we also provide some composition-like operations.

    Finally, we provide the Ring structure on AddMonoid.End.

    Range of linear maps #

    The range LinearMap.range of a (semi)linear map f : M ‚Üí M‚ÇÇ is a submodule of M‚ÇÇ.

    More specifically, LinearMap.range applies to any SemilinearMapClass over a RingHomSurjective ring homomorphism.

    Note that this also means that dot notation (i.e. f.range for a linear map f) does not work.

    Notation #

    • We continue to use the notations M ‚Üí‚Çõ‚Çó[œÉ] M‚ÇÇ and M ‚Üí‚Çó[R] M‚ÇÇ for the type of semilinear (resp. linear) maps from M to M‚ÇÇ over the ring homomorphism œÉ (resp. over the ring R).

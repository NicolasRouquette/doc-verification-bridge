    Localization of Submodules #

    Results about localizations of submodules and quotient modules are provided in this file.

    Main results #

    • Submodule.localized: The localization of an R-submodule of M at p viewed as an R‚Çö-submodule of M‚Çö. A direct consequence of this is that R‚Çö is flat over R; see IsLocalization.flat.
    • Submodule.toLocalized: The localization map of a submodule M' ‚Üí‚Çó[R] M'.localized p.
    • Submodule.toLocalizedQuotient: The localization map of a quotient module M ‚ß∏ M' ‚Üí‚Çó[R] LocalizedModule p M ‚ß∏ M'.localized p.

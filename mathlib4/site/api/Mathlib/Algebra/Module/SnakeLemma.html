    The snake lemma in terms of modules #

    The snake lemma is proven in Mathlib/Algebra/Homology/ShortComplex/SnakeLemma.lean for all abelian categories, but for definitional equality and universe issues we reprove them here for modules.

    Main results #

    • SnakeLemma.Œ¥: The connecting homomorphism guaranteed by the snake lemma.
    • SnakeLemma.exact_Œ¥_left: The connecting homomorphism is exact on the right.
    • SnakeLemma.exact_Œ¥_right: The connecting homomorphism is exact on the left.

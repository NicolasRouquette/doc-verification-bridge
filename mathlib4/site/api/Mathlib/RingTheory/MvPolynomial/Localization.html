    Localization and multivariate polynomial rings #

    In this file we show some results connecting multivariate polynomial rings and localization.

    Main results #

    • MvPolynomial.isLocalization: If S is the localization of R at a submonoid M, then MvPolynomial œÉ S is the localization of MvPolynomial œÉ R at the image of M in MvPolynomial œÉ R.

    Faithfully flat modules #

    A module M over a commutative ring R is faithfully flat if it is flat and IM ‚â† M whenever I is a maximal ideal of R.

    Main declaration #

    • Module.FaithfullyFlat: the predicate asserting that an R-module M is faithfully flat.

    Main theorems #

    • Module.FaithfullyFlat.iff_flat_and_proper_ideal: an R-module M is faithfully flat iff it is flat and for all proper ideals I of R, I ‚Ä¢ M ‚â† M.

    • Module.FaithfullyFlat.iff_flat_and_rTensor_faithful: an R-module M is faithfully flat iff it is flat and tensoring with M is faithful, i.e. N ‚â† 0 implies N ‚äó M ‚â† 0.

    • Module.FaithfullyFlat.iff_flat_and_lTensor_faithful: an R-module M is faithfully flat iff it is flat and tensoring with M is faithful, i.e. N ‚â† 0 implies M ‚äó N ‚â† 0.

    • Module.FaithfullyFlat.iff_exact_iff_rTensor_exact: an R-module M is faithfully flat iff tensoring with M preserves and reflects exact sequences, i.e. the sequence N‚ÇÅ ‚Üí N‚ÇÇ ‚Üí N‚ÇÉ is exact iff the sequence N‚ÇÅ ‚äó M ‚Üí N‚ÇÇ ‚äó M ‚Üí N‚ÇÉ ‚äó M is exact.

    • Module.FaithfullyFlat.iff_exact_iff_lTensor_exact: an R-module M is faithfully flat iff tensoring with M preserves and reflects exact sequences, i.e. the sequence N‚ÇÅ ‚Üí N‚ÇÇ ‚Üí N‚ÇÉ is exact iff the sequence M ‚äó N‚ÇÅ ‚Üí M ‚äó N‚ÇÇ ‚Üí M ‚äó N‚ÇÉ is exact.

    • Module.FaithfullyFlat.iff_zero_iff_lTensor_zero: an R-module M is faithfully flat iff for all linear maps f : N ‚Üí N', f = 0 iff M ‚äó f = 0.

    • Module.FaithfullyFlat.iff_zero_iff_rTensor_zero: an R-module M is faithfully flat iff for all linear maps f : N ‚Üí N', f = 0 iff f ‚äó M = 0.

    • Module.FaithfullyFlat.of_linearEquiv: modules linearly equivalent to a flat modules are flat

    • Module.FaithfullyFlat.trans: if S is R-faithfully flat and M is S-faithfully flat, then M is R-faithfully flat.

    • Module.FaithfullyFlat.self: the R-module R is faithfully flat.

    Cosets #

    This file develops the basic theory of left and right cosets.

    When G is a group and a : G, s : Set G, with open scoped Pointwise we can write:

    • the left coset of s by a as a ‚Ä¢ s
    • the right coset of s by a as MulOpposite.op a ‚Ä¢ s (or op a ‚Ä¢ s with open MulOpposite, or s <‚Ä¢ a with open scoped Pointwise RightActions)

    If instead G is an additive group, we can write (with open scoped Pointwise still)

    • the left coset of s by a as a +·µ• s
    • the right coset of s by a as AddOpposite.op a +·µ• s (or op a +·µ• s with open AddOpposite, or s <+·µ• a with open scoped Pointwise RightActions)

    Main definitions #

    • QuotientGroup.quotient s: the quotient type representing the left cosets with respect to a subgroup s, for an AddGroup this is QuotientAddGroup.quotient s.
    • QuotientGroup.mk: the canonical map from Œ± to Œ±/s for a subgroup s of Œ±, for an AddGroup this is QuotientAddGroup.mk.

    Notation #

    • G ‚ß∏ H is the quotient of the (additive) group G by the (additive) subgroup H

    support of a permutation #

    Main definitions #

    In the following, f g : Equiv.Perm Œ±.

    • Equiv.Perm.Disjoint: two permutations f and g are Disjoint if every element is fixed either by f, or by g. Equivalently, f and g are Disjoint iff their support are disjoint.
    • Equiv.Perm.IsSwap: f = swap x y for x ‚â† y.
    • Equiv.Perm.support: the elements x : Œ± that are not fixed by f.

    Assume Œ± is a Fintype:

    • Equiv.Perm.fixed_point_card_lt_of_ne_one f says that f has strictly less than Fintype.card Œ± - 1 fixed points, unless f = 1. (Equivalently, f.support has at least 2 elements.)

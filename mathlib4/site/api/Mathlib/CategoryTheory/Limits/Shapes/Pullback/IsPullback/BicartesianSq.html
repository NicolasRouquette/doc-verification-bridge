    Bi-Cartesian squares #

    BicartesianSq f g h i is the proposition that

      W ---f---> X
  |          |
  g          h
  |          |
  v          v
  Y ---i---> Z

    is a pullback square and a pushout square.

    We show that the pullback and pushout squares for a biproduct are bi-Cartesian.

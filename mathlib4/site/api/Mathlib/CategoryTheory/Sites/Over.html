    Localization

    In this file, given a Grothendieck topology J on a category C and X : C, we construct a Grothendieck topology J.over X on the category Over X. In order to do this, we first construct a bijection Sieve.overEquiv Y : Sieve Y ‚âÉ Sieve Y.left for all Y : Over X. Then, as it is stated in SGA 4 III 5.2.1, a sieve of Y : Over X is covering for J.over X if and only if the corresponding sieve of Y.left is covering for J. As a result, the forgetful functor Over.forget X : Over X ‚•§ X is both cover-preserving and cover-lifting.

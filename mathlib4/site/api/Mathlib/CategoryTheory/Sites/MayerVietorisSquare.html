    Mayer-Vietoris squares #

    The purpose of this file is to allow the formalization of long exact Mayer-Vietoris sequences in sheaf cohomology. If X‚ÇÑ is an open subset of a topological space that is covered by two open subsets X‚ÇÇ and X‚ÇÉ, it is known that there is a long exact sequence ... ‚ü∂ H^q(X‚ÇÑ) ‚ü∂ H^q(X‚ÇÇ) ‚äû H^q(X‚ÇÉ) ‚ü∂ H^q(X‚ÇÅ) ‚ü∂ H^{q+1}(X‚ÇÑ) ‚ü∂ ... where X‚ÇÅ is the intersection of X‚ÇÇ and X‚ÇÉ, and H^q are the cohomology groups with values in an abelian sheaf.

    In this file, we introduce a structure GrothendieckTopology.MayerVietorisSquare which extends Square C, and asserts properties which shall imply the existence of long exact Mayer-Vietoris sequences in sheaf cohomology (TODO). We require that the map X‚ÇÅ ‚ü∂ X‚ÇÉ is a monomorphism and that the square in C becomes a pushout square in the category of sheaves after the application of the functor yoneda ‚ãô presheafToSheaf J _. Note that in the standard case of a covering by two open subsets, all the morphisms in the square would be monomorphisms, but this dissymmetry allows the example of Nisnevich distinguished squares in the case of the Nisnevich topology on schemes (in which case f‚ÇÇ‚ÇÑ : X‚ÇÇ ‚ü∂ X‚ÇÑ shall be an open immersion and f‚ÇÉ‚ÇÑ : X‚ÇÉ ‚ü∂ X‚ÇÑ an √©tale map that is an isomorphism over the closed (reduced) subscheme X‚ÇÑ - X‚ÇÇ, and X‚ÇÅ shall be the pullback of f‚ÇÇ‚ÇÑ and f‚ÇÉ‚ÇÑ.).

    Given a Mayer-Vietoris square S and a presheaf P on C, we introduce a sheaf condition S.SheafCondition P and show that it is indeed satisfied by sheaves.

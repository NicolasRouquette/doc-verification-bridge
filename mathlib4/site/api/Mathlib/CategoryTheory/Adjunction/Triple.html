    Adjoint triples #

    This file concerns adjoint triples F ‚ä£ G ‚ä£ H of functors F H : C ‚•§ D, G : D ‚•§ C. We first prove that F is fully faithful iff H is, and then prove results about the two special cases where G is fully faithful or F and H are.

    Main results #

    All results are about an adjoint triple F ‚ä£ G ‚ä£ H where adj‚ÇÅ : F ‚ä£ G and adj‚ÇÇ : G ‚ä£ H. We bundle the adjunctions in a structure Triple F G H.

    • fullyFaithfulEquiv: F is fully faithful iff H is.
    • rightToLeft: the canonical natural transformation H ‚ü∂ F that exists whenever G is fully faithful. This is defined as the preimage of adj‚ÇÇ.counit ‚â´ adj‚ÇÅ.unit under whiskering with G, but formulas in terms of the units resp. counits of the adjunctions are also given.
    • whiskerRight_rightToLeft: whiskering rightToLeft : H ‚ü∂ F with G yields adj‚ÇÇ.counit ‚â´ adj‚ÇÅ.unit : H ‚ãô G ‚ü∂ F ‚ãô G.
    • epi_rightToLeft_app_iff_epi_map_adj‚ÇÅ_unit_app: rightToLeft : H ‚ü∂ F is epic at X iff the image of adj‚ÇÅ.unit.app X under H is.
    • epi_rightToLeft_app_iff_epi_map_adj‚ÇÇ_counit_app: rightToLeft : H ‚ü∂ F is epic at X iff the image of adj‚ÇÇ.counit.app X under F is.
    • epi_rightToLeft_app_iff: when H preserves epimorphisms, rightToLeft : H ‚ü∂ F is epic at X iff adj‚ÇÇ.counit ‚â´ adj‚ÇÅ.unit : H ‚ãô G ‚ü∂ F ‚ãô G is.
    • leftToRight: the canonical natural transformation F ‚ü∂ H that exists whenever F and H are fully faithful. This is defined in terms of the units of the adjunctions, but a formula in terms of the counits is also given.
    • whiskerLeft_leftToRight: whiskering G with leftToRight : F ‚ü∂ H yields adj‚ÇÅ.counit ‚â´ adj‚ÇÇ.unit : G ‚ãô F ‚ü∂ G ‚ãô H.
    • mono_leftToRight_app_iff_mono_adj‚ÇÇ_unit_app: leftToRight : F ‚ü∂ H is monic at X iff adj‚ÇÇ.unit is monic at F.obj X.
    • mono_leftToRight_app_iff_mono_adj‚ÇÅ_counit_app: leftToRight : F ‚ü∂ H is monic at X iff adj‚ÇÅ.counit is monic at H.obj X.
    • mono_leftToRight_app_iff: leftToRight : F ‚ü∂ H is componentwise monic iff adj‚ÇÅ.counit ‚â´ adj‚ÇÇ.unit : G ‚ãô F ‚ü∂ G ‚ãô H is.

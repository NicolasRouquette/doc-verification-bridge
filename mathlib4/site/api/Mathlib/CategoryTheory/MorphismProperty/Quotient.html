    Classes of morphisms induced on quotient categories #

    Let W : MorphismProperty C and homRel : HomRel C. We assume that homRel is stable under pre- and postcomposition. We introduce a property W.HasQuotient homRel expressing that W induces a property of morphisms on the quotient category, i.e. W f ‚Üî W g when homRel f g holds. We denote W.quotient homRel : MorphismProperty (Quotient homRel) the induced property of morphisms: a morphism in C satisfies W iff (Quotient.functor homRel).map f does.

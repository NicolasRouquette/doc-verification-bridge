    Triangles in the opposite category of a (pre)triangulated category #

    Let C be a (pre)triangulated category. In CategoryTheory.Triangulated.Opposite.Basic, we have constructed a shift on C·µí·µñ that will be part of a structure of (pre)triangulated category. In this file, we construct an equivalence of categories between (Triangle C)·µí·µñ and Triangle C·µí·µñ, called CategoryTheory.Pretriangulated.triangleOpEquivalence.

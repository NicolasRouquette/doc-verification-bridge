    Hilbert space for quantum mechanics on Space d #

    source
    @[reducible, inline]
    abbrev QuantumMechanics.SpaceDHilbertSpace (d : ℕ) :
    AddSubgroup (Space d →ₘ[MeasureTheory.volume] ℂ)

    The Hilbert space for single-particle quantum mechanics on Space d is defined to be L²(Space d, ℂ), the space of almost-everywhere equal equivalence classes of square-integrable functions from Space d to ℂ.

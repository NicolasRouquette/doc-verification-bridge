    𝓕.WickAlgebra →ₗ[ℂ] 𝓕.WickAlgebra

    For a field specification 𝓕, timeOrder is the linear map

    WickAlgebra 𝓕 →ₗ[ℂ] WickAlgebra 𝓕

    defined as the descent of ι ∘ₗ timeOrderF : FieldOpFreeAlgebra 𝓕 →ₗ[ℂ] WickAlgebra 𝓕 from FieldOpFreeAlgebra 𝓕 to WickAlgebra 𝓕. This descent exists because ι ∘ₗ timeOrderF is well-defined on equivalence classes.

    The notation 𝓣(a) is used for timeOrder a.

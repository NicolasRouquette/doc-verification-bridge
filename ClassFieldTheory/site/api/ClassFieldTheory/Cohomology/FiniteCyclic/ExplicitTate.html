    Explicit description of the 0-th and -1-st Tate cohomology groups for finite cyclic groups #

    In this file, we provide an explicit description of the 0-th and -1-st Tate cohomology groups of a representation of a finite cyclic group in terms of its norm map.

    For a representation A of a finite cyclic group G generated by g : G, this is the map x ↦ x - g • x.

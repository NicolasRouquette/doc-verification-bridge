    Let M : Rep R G, where G is a finite cyclic group. We construct an exact sequence

    0 ⟶ M ⟶ coind₁'.obj M ⟶ ind₁'.obj M ⟶ M ⟶ 0.

    In fact, we construct this as two exact short complexes

    periodSeq₁Functor : Rep R G ⥤ ShortComplex (Rep R G) sending M to the exact complex periodSeq₁ M : M ⟶ coind₁'.obj M ⟶ ind₁'.obj M and periodSeq₂Functor : Rep R G ⥤ ShortComplex (Rep R G) sending M to the exact complex periodSeq₂ M : coind₁'.obj M ⟶ ind₁'.obj M ⟶ M

    Using this sequence, we construct an isomorphism

    dimensionShift.up.obj M ≅ dimensionShift.down.obj M.

    Using this, construct isomorphisms

    Hⁿ⁺¹(G,M) ≅ Hⁿ⁺³(G,M).
